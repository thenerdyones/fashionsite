About Us

In 2004, the visionary Augusto Macado Varela Gomes embarked on a journey that would redefine the fabric landscape in the UK. Born into a family with a rich heritage in textiles, Augusto inherited a passion for quality craftsmanship and a keen eye for design. Inspired by the vibrant tapestries of his upbringing, he envisioned a company that would blend tradition with contemporary flair. Augusto's odyssey began in the quaint town of Bath, where he decided to establish Gomes1 Fashion. Fuelled by a desire to share the beauty of his family's textile legacy, he meticulously curated a collection that reflected his diverse cultural influences. Each fabric told a story, weaving together the threads of history, innovation, and personal connection. Determined to offer more than just fabrics, Augusto envisioned Gomes1 Fashion as a creative hub, fostering collaboration between designers and artists. The company quickly became a melting pot of ideas, attracting talents from all corners of the UK. Augusto's commitment to supporting creativity and craftsmanship transformed Gomes1 Fashion into a beloved institution within the textile community. Over the years, Gomes1 Fashion expanded its reach, becoming a symbol of British textile excellence. Augusto's unwavering dedication to quality, coupled with his innate ability to forecast trends, established Gomes1 Fashion as a trendsetter in the industry. As the legacy of Gomes1 Fashion continued to flourish, Augusto Macado Varela Gomes' name became synonymous with innovation, creativity, and a profound love for textiles. Today, the company stands as a testament to his vision, welcoming enthusiasts and professionals alike to experience the magic of fabrics woven with passion and purpose.
